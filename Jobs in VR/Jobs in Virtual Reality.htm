What is VR?


Virtual reality is a simulated experience that can be similar to or completely different from the real world. Applications of virtual reality can include entertainment and educational purposes. Other, distinct types of VR style technology include augmented reality and mixed reality.

Jobs in VR


The top VR Employers

Oculus

Oculus is a company that manufactures Virtual Reality headsets, and a subsidiary company of Facebook Inc. Oculus Rift started a Kickstarter campaign to fund development and was later bought by Facebook, making them one of the leading manufacturers in the VR world.

Amazon Co. VR

Amazon is one of, if not the leading delivery services that recently started testing the waters in the world of Virtual Reality. Amazon offers jobs in the developer field and is seeking out mid-senior level developers to join their newly birthed branch in VR.

Unity

Unity VR lets you target virtual reality devices directly from Unity, without any external plugins in projects. It provides a base API and feature set with compatibility for multiple devices. It has been designed to provide forward compatibility for future devices and software.
